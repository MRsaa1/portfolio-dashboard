About

Senior Banking Executive | Ex-PrivatBank | Treasury, Liquidity, Risk & Interbank Operations

FinTech and AI Systems Architect with 19+ years of executive experience in banking and liquidity management at PrivatBank, Ukraine's largest systemically important bank. Led a team of 64 professionals managing daily turnover of up to UAH 2 billion ($50M equivalent), directing nationwide vault & CIT logistics across 32 cash centers, and orchestrating interbank transactions with the National Bank of Ukraine.

Proven expertise in liquidity planning, risk management & compliance, ATM cash forecasting, and process automation. Developed automated deal-confirmation systems that reduced cycle time by 90%, demonstrating hands-on capability in building Python-based analytical platforms, reporting dashboards, and Telegram bots for financial analytics and decision support.

Bridges academic research and applied innovation as a published author in SSRN (Emerging Markets Finance, Risk Management, Monetary Policy eJournals), with publications on cash infrastructure, liquidity management, and intelligent financial systems. Certified professional: CFA/AFP, DACFP (CBDA). Combines executive leadership experience with hands-on AI engineering and automation expertise, specializing in risk modeling and intelligent financial infrastructure.
